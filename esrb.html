ESRB Ratings Information

The table below contains the meaning of each definition of ESRB ratings

ESRB Rating Description
EC Early childhood: appropriate for ages 3+
E Everyone: suitable for ages 6+. May contain mild violence, language, and minor suggestive themes
E10+ Everyone 10+: suitable for ages 10+. May contain more mild violence, language, and minor suggestive themes
T Teen: suitable for ages 13+. May contain violence, infrequent use of strong language, crude humor, minimal blood, and/or simulated gambling
M Mature: suitable for ages 17+. May contain intense violence, blood and gore, sexual content, and/or strong language
AO Adults only: suitable for only ages 18+. May contain prolonged scenes of intense violence and/or graphic sexual content and nudity
RP Rating Pending: Submitted to ESRB, awaiting final rating. Only appears in advertising before a game is released

For more information on the ESRB ratings and content descriptions please visit http://www.esrb.org
