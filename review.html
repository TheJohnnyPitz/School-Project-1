Game Reviews

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare Reflex

7/10: A port of the 360/PS3 version of CoD4. The graphics have been downgraded and some game modes have been left out, but everything else is pretty much the same. The 360/PS3 versions are great games themselves.

Call of Duty: Black Ops

8/10: Solid first person shooter. The graphics are fantastic and the multiplayer gameplay is addicting. The single player campaign is repetitive, but still fun.

Super Smash Brothers: Brawl

9.5/10: Nearly flawless. A gret arcade fighter style game with great graphics, and a multiplayer mode that lasts forever. This is a good game to play with friends, but the online portion of the game is unfortunately bogged down with framerate issue.
